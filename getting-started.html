Getting Started

1.

Find out what is Evaluative Thinking?

We have developed simple questions that will help embed evaluative thinking from the get go. The questions are useful to ask when you plan, do and review your project, programme or any initiative.

2.

Understand what the Theory of Change is

We have a video animation explaining what a Theory of Change is. The Logic Model web app will also help you create when you are starting a project, programme or any initiative. We also have examples for you to look at.

3.

Check out Evaluation Tools & Resources

We have developed tools and resources that will help you when you plan, do and review for programme, project or any initiative, develop a logic model with our web app, video explaining what is a Theory of Change is and some example of what it looks like, some templates and guidance when you are asked to develop a policy or undertake an evaluation.

4.

Learn about our champions programme

The Evaluation Champions programme is part of addressing the challenge of embedding a culture of continuous learning across the Ministry. It is focused on empowering and encouraging people to practice evaluative thinking in their daily life.

5.

Considering commissioning a policy or evaluation

Do you get asked to draft a new policy or get an evaluation done? We have provided a simple and pragmatic process and template that you can use or adapt. We also have an assessment tool and guidance on how to make the evaluation useful and high quality.
